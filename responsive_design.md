Layout strategies

### Fixed-width design



Fixed-width layouts are best when

#### Pros

- Most similar to a static comp, therefore easest to design and implement.
- Easiest to maintain

#### Cons

- Doesn't take advantage of large screens
- Requires horizontal scrolling on small screens
